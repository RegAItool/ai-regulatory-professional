Regulatory work is 70% retrieval. We built the modules that automate it.

Classification, applicable standards mapping, predicate identification, intended use validation — these tasks consume 70% of a regulatory consultant's time, and most of a startup's $30,000-50,000 consulting bill.

None of this work benefits from senior human judgment. It benefits from systematic retrieval across FDA databases, ISO/IEC catalogs, and MDR guidance corpora. AI does this better, faster, and cheaper.

We published the underlying methodology (ESWA 2026, JMIR AI 2024, arXiv:2506.18511) so buyers can verify rigor before purchasing.

This practice delivers the tools as self-serve AI modules — and the expert layer as advisory services when judgment is required.
